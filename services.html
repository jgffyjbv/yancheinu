Coaching & Courses

Find the path that fits your journey

Yancheinu meets you where you are — with private one-on-one coaching for shidduchim and for life’s challenging seasons, acclaimed courses for mothers and for shadchanim, and inspiring speaking engagements. Different doors, one destination: clarity, confidence, and menuchas hanefesh.

Private & Confidential

One-on-One Coaching

Mrs. S. Eisenbach offers personalized shidduch coaching for parents and singles, with a thoughtful, individualized approach that helps clients move from confusion to clarity, confidence, and menuchas hanefesh.

Combining Torah-based hashkafah, intensive ICF coaching training, and decades of experience in sensitive shidduchim, she guides clients through the process with discretion, compassion, and practical support. Every conversation is completely confidential.

Book a Private Session

Support may include…

  • Gaining clarity on what truly matters in a shidduch
  • Handling delicate and sensitive situations with wisdom
  • Knowing how — and whether — to move forward at each stage of the dating process
  • Working effectively and respectfully with shadchanim
  • Inquiring properly — and knowing whom to trust for references
  • Staying calm and grounded through the ups and downs of the process
Beyond Shidduchim

1:1 Life Coaching

Alongside shidduch coaching, Mrs. S. Eisenbach offers private life coaching — with professional training through the ICF, the International Coaching Federation — working with individuals across ages, stages, and backgrounds.

When a mother is navigating a challenging season — whether emotional, mental, or practical — the shidduch journey can feel heavier. The decisions feel bigger; the pressure feels sharper. With the right support, women rediscover their calm, their confidence, and their ability to guide their children with love and clarity.

Book a Life-Coaching Session

Life coaching can help you…

  • Navigate a challenging season — emotional, mental, or practical
  • Understand each stage of the process you’re in
  • Make grounded decisions instead of pressured ones
  • Strengthen the family dynamic, even when life feels complicated
  • Rediscover your calm, confidence, and clarity
  • End every session with a clear, actionable next step

Yancheinu Courses — for Mothers & for Shadchanim

Structured, practical training that turns uncertainty into a clear plan — whether you’re guiding your own child or building shidduchim for others.

For Mothers of Children in the Parsha

Does this sound familiar? Questions many mothers find themselves asking…

  • “My daughter isn’t interested in shidduchim yet, and most of her friends aren’t engaged. Should we encourage her to begin — or give it more time?”
  • “My child refuses to meet a suggested shidduch because of the picture, but we feel it has strong potential. How should we handle it?”
  • “After the first date or bashow, my child is unsure. What now?”
  • “My husband and I aren’t on the same page — we’re looking for different qualities. How do we move forward together?”
  • “Should I involve my other children?”
  • “How do I make inquiries — and whom can I trust?”

You don’t have to navigate these questions alone. This course provides the clarity, guidance, and practical tools to help you move forward with confidence and menuchas hanefesh.

For Shadchanim

Training and empowerment for shadchanim — whether you’re just beginning or have years of experience. The course delivers practical tools, proven strategies, and professional guidance drawn from Mrs. S. Eisenbach’s 30+ years of experience in the shidduch world.
