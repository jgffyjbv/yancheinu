Coaching & Courses

Find the path that fits your journey

Yancheinu meets you where you are — with private one-on-one coaching for shidduchim and for life’s challenging seasons, acclaimed courses for mothers and for shadchanim, and inspiring speaking engagements. Different doors, one destination: clarity, confidence, and menuchas hanefesh.

Private & Confidential

One-on-One Coaching

Mrs. Eisenbach’s private sessions are highly sought after for their individualized, thoughtful approach — for parents navigating the parsha with their children, and for singles, including dating coaching.

Combining Torah-based hashkafah, intensive ICF coaching training, and decades of experience managing delicate and sensitive shidduchim, she guides every client from confusion to clarity — so you can move forward with confidence and menuchas hanefesh. Every conversation is completely confidential.

  • Handling delicate and sensitive situations with wisdom
  • Deciding how to proceed after a first date or bashow
  • Working effectively and respectfully with shadchanim

  • Staying calm and grounded through the ups and downs of the process
Beyond Shidduchim

1:1 Life Coaching

Alongside shidduch coaching, Mrs. Eisenbach offers private life coaching — with professional training through the ICF, the International Coaching Federation — working with individuals across ages, stages, and backgrounds.

When a mother is navigating a challenging season — whether emotional, mental, or practical — the shidduch journey can feel heavier. The decisions feel bigger; the pressure feels sharper. With the right support, women rediscover their calm, their confidence, and their ability to guide their children with love and clarity.

Book a Life-Coaching Session

Life coaching can help you…

  • Navigate a challenging season — emotional, mental, or practical
  • Understand each stage of the process you’re in
  • Make grounded decisions instead of pressured ones
  • Strengthen the family dynamic, even when life feels complicated
  • Rediscover your calm, confidence, and clarity
  • End every session with a clear, actionable next step
Acclaimed Programs

Yancheinu Courses — for Mothers & for Shadchanim

Structured, practical training that turns uncertainty into a clear plan — whether you’re guiding your own child or building shidduchim for others.

For Mothers of Children in the Parsha

So many mothers begin this often-overwhelming journey feeling unsure, unprepared, or uncertain. This course provides the clarity, guidance, and practical tools to navigate shidduchim with confidence and menuchas hanefesh — addressing the real questions mothers face:

  • When — and how — to begin
  • Pictures and resumes
  • What to do after the first date
  • When parents don’t agree
  • Involving your other children
  • How to inquire — and whom to trust

For Shadchanim

Training and empowerment for shadchanim — whether you’re just beginning or have years of experience. The course delivers practical tools, proven strategies, and professional guidance drawn from Mrs. Eisenbach’s 30+ years of experience in the shidduch world.

Participants learn how to work more skillfully with families and singles, handle sensitive information with discretion, and build the kind of trust that leads to more shidduchim — and better shidduchim.
