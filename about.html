About

Meet Mrs. S. Eisenbach

“Hi, I’m Simy — welcome to Yancheinu.”

For over 30 years, Mrs. S. Eisenbach has had the zechus to work in the shidduch world — guiding parents, singles, and shadchanim. That depth of experience led her to found Yancheinu (ינחנו), where she brings it together with professional ICF life-coaching training — guiding clients from confusion to clarity.

Why Yancheinu was born

A maze needs a Waze

“Navigating shidduchim is like a maze — we need a Waze!”

Through decades of guiding many parents, singles, and shadchanim, Mrs. S. Eisenbach saw a real need for clarity and direction. That is what led her to start Yancheinu (ינחנו) — a place where mothers, shadchanim, and singles find real answers instead of guesswork.

She is passionate about helping families approach shidduchim the way they deserve to — with confidence, direction, and true menuchas hanefesh.

The coaching

An individualized, thoughtful approach

Her private coaching sessions are highly sought after — because no two families, and no two shidduchim, are ever the same.

Combining Torah-based hashkafah, endorsed and recommended by leading rabbanim and roshei yeshivah, alongside the wisdom of decades navigating sensitive and complex shidduchim — and professional life-coaching training through the ICF, the International Coaching Federation — Mrs. S. Eisenbach works with individuals across ages, stages, and backgrounds, helping every client move from confusion to clarity, with confidence and menuchas hanefesh.

Torah-Based Hashkafah

Every piece of guidance is rooted in Torah values and the mesorah of how a bayis ne’eman is built — never imported from the outside world.

Decades of Experience

Over 30 years navigating sensitive and complex shidduchim — the kind of seasoned judgment that only comes from walking this road with countless families.

ICF Life-Coaching Training

Professional life-coaching training through the International Coaching Federation — a framework that turns warmth and wisdom into real, lasting clarity, for clients across ages, stages, and backgrounds.

Beyond shidduchim — life coaching

When life feels heavier, the parsha does too

“In my work, I’ve learned that when anyone is navigating a challenging season — whether emotional, mental, or practical — the shidduch journey can feel heavier. The decisions feel bigger. The pressure feels sharper. The fear of ‘messing up’ feels louder. But I’ve also seen how, with the right support, people rediscover their calm, their confidence, and their ability to move forward with clarity and peace of mind.”

That is why Yancheinu offers 1:1 life coaching — professional, ICF-trained guidance to help you navigate life’s complicated moments, strengthen personal relationships, and make grounded, thoughtful decisions with confidence, b’ezras Hashem.

Empowering mothers, shadchanim, and singles
