Meet Mrs. S. Eisenbach

Three decades of walking this road with families

For over 30 years, Mrs. S. Eisenbach has had the zechus to work in the shidduch world — guiding parents, singles, and shadchanim through the parsha. Through that experience she saw a real need for clarity and direction, and that is what led her to found Yancheinu (ינחנו).

She brings that depth of experience together with professional life-coaching training through the ICF — International Coaching Federation, working with individuals across ages, stages, and backgrounds — so every client can move forward with confidence and menuchas hanefesh.
